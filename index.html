Certainly! You have the opportunity to engage in multiple communities without any restrictions that might hinder the development and representation of your skills and talents.

Your academic commitments take precedence; events are planned with your academics in mind. You have the flexibility to decline work if academic concerns arise.

Starting this year, students from various branches can effortlessly join our community. Their inclusion is a testament to our community's success, earned through dedicated effort.

To become a core member, the selection process involves two rounds. The first is for shortlisting based on skills, followed by an interview round. Successful candidates proceed to join the core team.
